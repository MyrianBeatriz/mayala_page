nmap notes: SYN scan & service discovery

Published: 2025-10-05 • Category: Hacking

Only test within explicit scope and permission.

sudo nmap -Pn -p1-65535 -sS -sV -T4 example.com
  • -Pn Skip host discovery (assume host up)
  • -p1-65535 Scan all TCP ports
  • -sS Half-open SYN scan (fast/stealthier)
  • -sV Service/version detection
  • -T4 Faster timing (noisier)
